# TalentPilot AI — Privacy Notes (Québec/Canada)

## Scope
This app processes recruitment data (PII and HR-sensitive information).

## Québec Law 25 (high-level practices)
- Limit collection to necessary information
- Protect confidentiality (access control + audit)
- Provide transparency (privacy notice)
- Access / correction requests
- Retention & deletion policy

## PIPEDA (high-level practices)
- Consent and transparency
- Safeguards (technical + organizational)
- Accountability and breach response

## AI provider considerations
- AI prompts may include PII
- Prefer minimization/anonymization where possible
- Document subprocessors and data residency considerations

## Recommended documents to publish
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Data retention policy
- Subprocessor list (Supabase, Vercel, Anthropic)
- Security overview (high-level)
